Relocation Services

  • Assistance with finding and renting housing.
  • Help with job placement, connecting immigrants to local labor markets based on their skills.
  • Support for opening bank accounts and improving credit scores.

Family Services

  • School registration assistance for children in the USA.
  • Guidance on obtaining health insurance.
  • Organizing intellectual development programs for children (e.g., chess, coding, and art clubs).

Membership Plans

Users can choose from different service packages (basic and full) that cater to their specific needs, such as airport pickup, transportation, and housing assistance.

Psychological and Social Support

Providing psychological services to help immigrants cope with the challenges of relocation.

Interactive Features

  • A seamless user experience with smooth navigation.
  • Forms for service registration, sticky navigation, and mobile-friendly design.
  • Options for real-time updates on services such as job placement and housing availability.

Scalability

While the website initially targets Azerbaijani and Turkish immigrants, it is designed to be scalable, offering services to a broader audience, including Latin American, African, Asian, and European immigrant communities.
